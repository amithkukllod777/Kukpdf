# KukPDF — Competitor Selection

**Category:** Mobile (Android) PDF scanner + PDF-tools app · **Target users:** consumers + SMBs, India-first, global · **Business model:** free (KukLabs ecosystem, one shared account) · **Pricing segment:** free vs. freemium incumbents · **Checked:** 2026-07-14, Android scope.

> Evidence note: Google Play listings and vendors' marketing pages returned HTTP 403 to automated fetching. Claims rest on official help/subscription pages where reachable + web-search summaries that quote official listings. Dominant label = **PARTIALLY VERIFIED**. KukPDF's own capabilities = **SPEC** (internal, not externally verified).

## Selected competitors

| # | Competitor | Type | Why relevant |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **CamScanner** | Market leader (scanner) / Direct | Dominant mobile scanner in India/Asia (~300M self-reported). Same core loop (scan→enhance→OCR→PDF) + watermark, e-sign, password. The app KukPDF users switch **from**. |
| 2 | **Adobe Scan** | Market leader (brand/quality) / Direct | Free download, best-in-class OCR/edge detection, huge install base, "trusted default," now AI (Assistant + summarize). Sets the quality + AI bar. |
| 3 | **iLovePDF (mobile)** | Direct / Close substitute | Closest match to KukPDF's **tool breadth** — scanner + merge/split/compress/watermark/page-numbers/sign/add+remove-password/organize. India traction. |

## Excluded (with reason)
- **Microsoft Lens** — being **retired** (removed from stores ~mid-Nov 2025, users pushed to M365 Copilot; *PARTIALLY VERIFIED*). Auditing a discontinued app as live would mislead; also scanner-only.
- **Smallpdf** — web/desktop-first; mobile scanner secondary; Western-SMB positioning. iLovePDF is the stronger, India-relevant representative of the same "tools-suite-on-mobile" class; including both is redundant (*INFERRED*).
